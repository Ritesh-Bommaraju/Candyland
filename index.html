WHAT ARE WE ?

Welcome to Candyland, where colossal candyfloss constructions meld with a tantalizing taffy twistedness.Explore our sprawling sanctuary of confectionery bliss and be transported to an imaginative wonderland with a one-of-a-kind full sensory experience. This interactive art installation celebrates the vibrant colors and flavors of your favorite sugarcoated delights across over a dozen environments, from flying unicorn pigs to a marshmallow tsunami.
